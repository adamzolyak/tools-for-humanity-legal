Constellations App Alpha Testing

Hi! You've been invited to be an alpha tester for the Constellations App. You can learn more at constellationsapp.com.

I've been using the same principles and practices found in the Constellations App in my own personal journaling and wellness routine for the past few years. It's been so helpful to my own wellness that I wanted to share these tools with more people.

What's in it for me?

Alpha testers will be the first handful of people to use the Constellations App. It's a chance to shape the future of Constellations App, helping to make it useful so that it can positively impact many more people. While not guaranteed, your feedback could show up in a future version of the app!

Alpha testers will also receive 1 free year of Constellations App (assuming it becomes generally available on the Apple App Store) for 1 year after it's launched.

What's involved?

Being an alpha tester includes downloading the Constellations App (iOS only right now - iPhone and iPad), using it, and providing feedback to a few questions (see below) via email or in real time (ex. Zoom). You can also optionally provide additional feedback on future updates or whenever you have feedback to share. Lastly, you may also be asked to provide feedback on future updates and ideas.

Alpha testers may encounter bugs and issues, and their data may be lost due to bugs or updates. Alpha testers agree to use Constellation App "as is" with no warranty or remedy due to bugs, issues, or other unforeseen consequences.

You can be removed as an alpha tester at any time by emailing feedback@constellationsapp.com.

I'm in! What do I do?

If you're willing to be an alpha tester, please do the following:

  1. Request an invitation at https://constellationsapp.com/.
  2. You'll receive an invitation from Apple within 24 hours. After clicking the link, you might need to install TestFlight before being able to install the Constellation App. TestFlight is Apple's tool for distributing test builds of apps.
  3. Use the Constellations App for a few days.

Share your feedback, answering the questions below. Feel free to share your feedback via email at feedback@constellationsapp.com or contact me however works for you. I'd be happy to chat in real-time if that's easier for you. Thanks!

  • What is most compelling?
  • What is least compelling?
  • What's confusing?
  • What questions do you still have?
  • What other ideas or potential do you see?
  • What similar or related wellness practices have been useful to you?
  • What else?

I'm not in.

That's ok. Thanks for listening. If you know someone who might be interested, feel free to forward this email to them or email their contact info at feedback@constellationsapp.com.
